# Challenge 21 — Study Checklist

To solve this challenge, you should understand:
- `cProfile` + `pstats`: reading a profile, cumulative vs total time
- `timeit` / `timeit.repeat` and why you take the minimum
- Big-O analysis of nested loops and list `in` scans
- Hash-based grouping with dicts and sets
- Why `startswith` semantics differ from exact-prefix grouping (short keys!)
- Dict insertion order as observable behavior
- The "change the algorithm, not the constants" profiling signature
